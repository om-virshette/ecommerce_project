Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: July 24, 2025

Overview

At ElectroCart, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not satisfied, we're happy to offer returns and refunds within the guidelines below.

Return Eligibility

14-Day Return Window: Items must be returned within 14 days of delivery
Original Condition: Products must be unused, in original packaging with all tags and accessories
Proof of Purchase: Order number or receipt required
Some products like software, earphones, and personalized items may not be eligible for return due to hygiene or licensing reasons.

Refund Process

1. Return Initiation

Request a return through your account or contact our support team within 14 days

2. Return Approval

We'll email you return instructions within 24 business hours

3. Ship Your Return

Package the item securely and ship it back to us within 5 days of approval

4. Inspection

We inspect returned items (typically within 3 business days of receipt)

5. Refund Issued

Refund processed to original payment method within 5-7 business days

Refunds may take 7-10 business days to appear in your account depending on your bank's processing time.

Return Shipping

Free Returns: We provide prepaid return labels for defective or incorrect items
Customer-Paid Returns: For change of mind returns, customer bears return shipping costs
Recommendation: Use trackable shipping and retain proof of postage

Non-Returnable Items

  • Software, games, and digital downloads
  • Personal care items (earphones, etc.)
  • Customized or personalized products
  • Items damaged by misuse

Need Help With a Return?

Our customer support team is happy to assist you with any return questions.
