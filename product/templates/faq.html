Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days after processing. Express shipping delivers in 2-3 business days. Processing time is typically 1-2 business days.

Do you offer India shipping?

Currently, we only ship within Maharashtra. We plan to expand in Other states in the future.

Payment Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, Net Banking, and popular wallets like Paytm and PhonePe.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We never store your full payment details.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

You may return most items within 15 days of delivery. Items must be unused with original packaging. See our Return Policy for details.

Yes, we only sell 100% authentic products from authorized suppliers and manufacturers.
